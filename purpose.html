TeamBook's Purpose

I'm an avid Football (soccer) watcher.

However, I can't watch all the games on time and miss out.

There are highlight videos on YouTube but sometimes even they take out the fun by spoiling the score in the thumbnail or title (ESPN booo!).

So I took a problem I had and many others like myself in ESPN's comment sections had and turned it into a solution in the form of a website. Now I can:

  1. Select a Team from Top Leagues
  2. View Past and Future Matches by the selected Team< (without spoilers)/li>
  3. Watch Highlight of a game played by a team


I know you guys said you didn't want API. However, I wanted to work on a problem that I had and that needed live updates that API could satisfy. It still accomplishes the requirements of add, delete, sort. Now I have a website that others and I can genuinely use.
